Car Trip Song

finalized for April 2026 Drawing Club

keep a little secret in the back of the car
don't know for how long and
we're travelling far

lean over your shoulder just to hear what i say
don't know what you think and
don't know how you play

pull over for dinner and you can't even eat
your stomach's too full and
your tongue is too beat

sit alone in quiet for the rest of the ride
don't know what you heard and
can't tell if you cried
